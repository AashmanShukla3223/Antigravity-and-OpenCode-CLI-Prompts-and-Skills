# 🌓 THE DUALITY OF GLASS
In the Tahoe ecosystem, windows are not static overlays; they are Optical Prisms that react to the luminance and chroma of the underlying wallpaper.

# 🔆 LIGHT MODE: "SILICON CRYSTAL"
Surface: 85% Translucency.

Color Extraction: The glass picks up the warmest tones from the wallpaper and injects them into the window background.

Refraction: A 30px background blur that creates a "frosted ice" effect.

Shadows: High-diffusion, light gray shadows with a 0.1 opacity to simulate depth without weight.

Interaction: When a window moves over a bright area of the wallpaper, the text contrast automatically shifts to Deep Obsidian.

# 🌙 DARK MODE: "OBSIDIAN LIQUID"
Surface: 75% Translucency.

Color Extraction: The glass absorbs the coolest tones (blues/purples) from the wallpaper to create a "Deep Sea" effect.

Refraction: A 50px background blur, creating a denser, more "viscous" glass appearance.

Shadows: Wide, "Neon-Glow" shadows that take on the primary color of the wallpaper (e.g., a blue wallpaper creates a soft blue shadow glow).

Interaction: When a window moves over a dark area, a 1px Silicon Rim Light (White, 0.2 opacity) appears around the window border to maintain edge definition.

# 🌪️ DYNAMIC INTERACTION RULES
1. THE "KINETIC BLUR"
As a window is dragged, the background blur intensity temporarily drops to 10px to save "Silicon GPU" cycles.

Once the window is released, the blur "settles" back to its full depth with a spring-loaded animation.

2. THE "TINT OVERFLOW"
If the wallpaper is a solid color, the glass will adopt a 10% saturation of that color.

If the wallpaper is a complex image (like the "Tahoe Peaks"), the glass will calculate the Average Ambient Color of the section directly beneath the window.

3. THE "NOTCH SHADOW"
The Tahoe Notch casts a subtle "Gravity Shadow" onto the wallpaper.

In Dark Mode, the Notch emits a soft "Neural Glow" (Silicon Pink) when an app like iPhone Mirroring is active.

# 🖼️ WALLPAPER CATEGORIES
# 🌌 THE "SILICON SURGE" (DEFAULT)
Visuals: Flowing liquid metal waves in iridescent purples and blues.

Interaction: Causes windows to look "Electric" and high-contrast.

# 🌲 THE "TAHOE PEAKS" (PHOTOGRAPHIC)
Visuals: High-resolution 8K photography of the Sierra Nevada.

Interaction: Causes windows to feel grounded, using natural grays and sky-blues for tinting.

# 🍎 THE "RESTAURANT RETRO" (LEGACY)
Visuals: Solid colors or gradients from the 1984/2001 eras.

Interaction: Simplifies glass refraction to a "Flat Era" style to respect the legacy aesthetics of the Windows XP or Apple II diners.
